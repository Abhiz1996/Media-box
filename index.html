Internal Communications Workflow

Media Box Request Hub

A single smart form for social media creatives, post-event posters, external events, achievements, and press releases. Every submission can be routed to a spreadsheet and prepared as a PDF questionnaire response.

Branching questionnaire PDF-ready output Google Sheets integration

Flow

  1. Employee selects department and request category.
  2. Only relevant questions appear.
  3. Submission can sync to Sheets and generate a PDF copy.

Your email address can be added later in the backend configuration without changing the form again.
